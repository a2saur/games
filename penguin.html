Instructions (Drop down)
Don't let your side get pooped on by the penguins!
Use the arrow keys (player 1) or A & D keys (player 2) to move your character to penguins that are about to poop.
Use the up arrow (player 1) or W key (player 2) to flip the penguins around so they'll hit your enemy instead of you!
You can go as long as you want, good luck!
