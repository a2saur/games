About The Creator

Welcome to A2 Games!
Please enjoy!

This is my website of games!
There are 3 groupings of games; Completed, Work in Progress, and Physics
Some of the Work in Progress games don't work, so they wouldn't be fun games to play.
The Physics category contains physics simulators. You can play around with them, but they aren't as polished.
Finally, the Completed category contains completed games that you can play, although they may get updated in the future. There are minigames and full games to play!

About the individual games

Field of Geese
In a field full of geese, how far can you get?
In this minigame, dodge geese for as long as you can!
-----Keys-----
Use the up, down, left, and right keys to move around.
Pressing a key starts moving in that direction stops it (ex: press up to start going up and down to stop going up)


A Goose's Field
The sequel to Field of Geese, this minigame uses the same keys to move around.
However, this time you're... a goose?
Try to catch the children (they will run away if you get too close!) and maybe they'll drop some good food...
-----Keys-----
Use the up, down, left, and right keys to move around.
Pressing a key starts moving in that direction stops it (ex: press up to start going up and down to stop going up)


Collection Game
In this game, you can explore the area and find various creatures!
There are 4 types of land: Mountain, Grass, Beach, and Water
You can find various towns in the game, and talk to the villagers as well as sell items in the shop.
Note: This game takes place on a randomly generated map! (Pretty cool right?)
-----Keys-----
Use the up, down, left, and right keys to move around.
Use the space key to select something (to talk to a villager or select an option) and the enter key to finish selling items in the shop


Penguin Poopers
Uh oh, these penguins keep pooping!
In this minigame, try to keep your side clean by turning the penguins when there about to poo!
The more times your side gets hit, the more dirty it gets!
Note: There is no end to the game, so play as long as you'd like!
-----Keys-----
Player 1: Use the left, and right keys to move around and up key to turn your penguin
Player 2: Use the a, and d keys to move around and w key to turn your penguin
